About Us

The CHILL program is a transformative journey designed specifically for young adults in South Africa. Our program is dedicated to empowering learners to achieve a harmonious balance between hard and soft skills with precision and effectiveness. Central to our approach is the introduction of the "GROWTH MINDSET" concept, which fosters self-reflection and introspection.

We align our program with the general aims of the South African Curriculum, specifically the CAPS curriculum, and tailor our content to each phase of learning. Our efforts are guided by the "National Curriculum Statement Grades R-12," which emphasizes the acquisition and application of knowledge and skills in ways that are meaningful to their lives. We commit to promoting knowledge within local contexts while remaining sensitive to global imperatives. Join us in this enriching experience, and let's embark on a journey of growth and empowerment together.
